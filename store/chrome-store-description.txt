Master French while browsing with French Popups. Hover over any French word on any webpage to see instant translations in 23+ languages. Our extension features a comprehensive database of 122,000+ words from WikDict and Wiktionary, all stored 100% offline for maximum privacy and lightning-fast speed. Easily activate or deactivate the extension globally from the popup menu, keeping it active only when you are in "study mode." Enjoy a clean, professional, and beautiful UI designed to help you learn without distraction. Perfect for students, travelers, and anyone looking to improve their French naturally while reading the web.

üöÄ HOW TO USE
1. Install the extension.
2. Click the extension icon in your toolbar and click "Activate Extension."
3. Navigate to any website containing French text.
4. Hover your mouse over any French word to see the instant translation popup.
5. Deactivate the extension globally when you finish your study session.
